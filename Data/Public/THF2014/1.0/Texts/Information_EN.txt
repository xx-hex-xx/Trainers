Function notes:
◦ Invulnerability – health is always at maximum, falls from great heights are safe and there is no damage animation;
◦ Infinite focus – focus is always maximum;
◦ Infinite gold – sets the gold amount to 10,000;
◦ Infinite arrows – the current number of arrows does not decrease;
◦ Infinite focus points – sets the number of focus points to 5;
◦ Infinite food – the current amount of food does not decrease;
◦ Infinite poppies – the current number of poppies does not decrease;
◦ Infinite flash bombs – the current number of flash bombs does not decrease;
◦ Infinite throwables – the current number of throwables does not decrease;
◦ Invisibility – enemies will ignore you;
◦ Easy lockpicking – does not work when lockpicking with focus with an upgraded skill;
◦ Fast movement speed ​​– sets the movement speed to 2.25;
◦ No clip mode – to control the character use standard movement keys. To go up use the Left Shift key, to go down use the Left Alt key.

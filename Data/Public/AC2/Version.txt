◦ The "Infinite underwater breath hold" function;
◦ The "Show collectibles" function.

◦ The "Invulnerability" function. No damage animation;
◦ The "Low enemies health" function. Enemies immediately have low health after taking damage;
◦ The "No notoriety" function. The current notoriety is instantly reset to zero;
◦ The "No armor break" function;
◦ The "Infinite allies health" function. Allies do not take damage from falling from heights;
◦ The "Teleport to custom marker" function;
◦ The "Kill civilians without desynchronization" function.

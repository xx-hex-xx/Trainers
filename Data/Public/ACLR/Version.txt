[Changes_EN]***Update: 04/05/2025 (version 1.3)***
The trainer structure has been changed, the configuration files will be recreated.
– Fixed:
◦ The "No notoriety" and "Maximum notoriety" function. Sometimes notoriety only changed visually.
– Changed:
◦ The "Infinite money" function. Now the value changes only for the player;
◦ The "Time of day" function. Now the time of day changes in all missions;
◦ The "No clip mode" function. Now works correctly when using the "Teleport to custom marker" function;
◦ The "No reload shoot" function. Also works when finishing off enemies.

***Update: 01/25/2025 (version 1.2)***
The trainer has been completely reworked.
– Added:
◦ The "Infinite arrows" function;
◦ The "No notoriety" function;
◦ The "Increase time of day (+1 hour)" function;
◦ The "Decrease time of day (-1 hour)" function;
◦ The "Maximum notoriety" function;
◦ The "No clip mode" function;
◦ The "Freeze everyone in place" function;
◦ The "Fast bodies/treasures loot" function;
◦ The "Fast steal" function;
◦ The "Infinite carriage health" function;
◦ The "Infinite raft health" function;
◦ The "Show collectibles" function;
◦ The "Full memory synchronization" function;
◦ The "Unfog map" function.
– Changed:
◦ The "Invulnerability" function. No damage animation;
◦ The "Invisibility" function. Enemies will lose sight of you during combat;
◦ The "Low enemies health" function. Enemies immediately have low health after taking damage;
◦ The "Infinite allies health" function. Affects all allies;
◦ The "Teleport to custom marker" function. Teleportation now works correctly for all map icons;
◦ The "Kill civilians without desynchronization" function.

***Update: 06/23/2022 (version 1.1)***
– Added:
◦ The "Infinite canoe health" function.
– Changed:
◦ The "Infinite underwater breath hold" function.[Changes]

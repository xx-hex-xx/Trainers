◦ The "Infinite lantern" function.

◦ The "Invulnerability" function. No animation from damage, health no longer drops to zero in some cases;

◦ The "Stop countdown timer" function. Also affects the timer while stalkering;

◦ The "Unfog current map" function.

– Changed:

Function notes:
◦ Invulnerability – health is always maximum, no damage animation;
◦ Infinite focus – focus is always maximum;
◦ Infinite mirian – sets the amount of mirian to 5,000. In order to see the effect you need to have at least one mirian;
◦ Infinite elf-shots – elf-shots is always maximum;
◦ Infinite ability points – sets the number of ability points to 5. In order to see the effect you need to have at least one ability point;
◦ Reveal captain/warchief data – to see the effect you need to go to the Sauron's Army menu and mark the target you need. Reveals the captain/warchief's identity and his strengths and weaknesses;
◦ Free abilities purchase – unlocks all abilities except story ones (to avoid bugs) and also allows you to buy them without having enough ability points;
◦ Only high-level runes drop – captains always drop level 26 runes and warchiefs always drop legendary runes;
◦ Invisibility – enemies will not pay attention to you;
◦ Stop countdown timer – the current timer in the quest and in additional objectives does not decrease;
◦ XP multiplier – multiplies the amount of experience gained by the value that you can select on the trackbar;
◦ Power multiplier – multiplies the amount of power gained by the value that you can select on the trackbar;
◦ Fast movement speed ​​– sets the movement speed to 2.25;
◦ Change time of day – changes the time of day in the following sequence: morning -> night -> day -> night -> evening -> rainy night, etc;
◦ No clip mode – to control the character use the W and S keys to move along the Y-axis (forward-backward), A and D to move along the X-axis (left-right). To go up use the Spacebar key, to go down use the Left Ctrl key.
